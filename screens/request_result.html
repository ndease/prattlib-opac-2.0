Your Request

You will receive an email to schedule your pick up time for this material at when it is ready.

What happens next...

We'll package it

Library staff will verify that the item is available and package it with any other items you requested for pickup.

You'll pick a time

When it's ready, you'll get an email to your @pratt.edu address with the subject, "Circulation Notice from Pratt Libraries." Important! This email has a link for you to schedule your pickup appointment time.

Swing by the library!

Once approved by library staff, you will receive another email confirming your pickup time! Please note that you must adhere to campus access requirements before coming on campus.
